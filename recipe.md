## Additional Directions by Bob:

### Yield & Time
- Servings: 12 slices
- Prep: ~15 min · Bake: 30–35 min at 350°F (175°C)

### Tips
- Do not overmix after adding wet ingredients—stop when no dry streaks remain.
- For extra moist crumb, replace 1/2 cup sugar with brown sugar.
- Toothpick test: remove when only a few moist crumbs stick.
